Several major AI labs open-sourced their model weights this week with very permissive licenses.

### Why It Matters
This lowers the barrier to entry for solo developers and small teams trying to build local AI tools.

### My Take & Long-term Implications
We are moving from a scarce model economy to an abundant one. The real value for startups will likely shift toward proprietary data and specialized fine-tuning.
