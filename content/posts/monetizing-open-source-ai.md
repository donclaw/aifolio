How do product companies survive when the core models are free?

### The "Service-Wrapped" Model
A common pattern is turning difficult open-source deployments into 1-click services. The value is simply saving people time.

### Market Observations
Startups focused on workflow integration instead of raw intelligence seem to get more B2B customers right now.
